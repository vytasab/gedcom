Genealogical data, or "family forest" web site (again invented a "bicycle)

It is designed to collect data, edit, view, or browse through them. It does not pretend to be better than the commercial genealogical data websites. It is a little different .
This site is not at all 'public', the site administrator vytasab@gmail.com decide who to sign it. All data manipulation operations are audited in the website.

Only visitors with essential information (name, surname and date of birth yyyy-MM-dd)  entered in the "family forest" can browse the website data . These visitors may readonly login the website:
          GEDCOM → Show me among the relatives
Svetainėje prisiregistravę asmenys turi teisę duomenis papildyti ir redaguoti. Jie svetainėn jungiasi:
The registered users have the rights to add /edit the data. They can login:
          Authentication → Login

The website data structure corresponds GEDCOM  http://homepages.rootsweb.ancestry.com/~pmcbride/gedcom/55gctoc.htm standard subset

The website data can be browsed in two ways:

  • family forest: you can "walk" in the family forest by mouse-clicking on the rectangle of next selected person. Five green arrows on the top left side of the screen allows to shift forest view, if it does not fit the whole screen. One person is highlighted in bold rectangular frame, move the mouse on the rectangle to position when rectangle color becomes richer, click mouse - and you can go surfing in
  • text mode: the page displays the selected person's immediate family: at the top - the parents, at the bottom - the family (or families) members. You can browse family info by clicking mouse on parents or family members name-surname link.. The registered users may add/edit the selected personal and his family event or attribute information. Click the icon  to back to family forest browsing

The site does not yet have the functionality of adoption.
